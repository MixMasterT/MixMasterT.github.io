Pancakes

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Soft, warm and delicious, pancakes make an excellent complement to any breakfast.

Although pancakes are very simple, they can easily be enhanced by varying the ingredients. For example, you can vary the types of flour, exchange fruit for some of the sugar...etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (all purpose) flour
  • 1 and 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 Tspoon butter
  • 3 and 1/2 tspoons baking powder
  • 1 tspoons salt
  • 1 Tspoon sugar
  • 1/4 tspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Directions

  1. Prepare batter

    First, mix dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Mix wet ingredients. Beat until fluffy. Fold wet ingredients over dry ingreedients. Mix gently until smooth. If necessary, add water to desired thickness.

  2. Cook Pancakes

    Prepare a pan or skillet to medium heat keep surface oiled or buttered. Use mug, ladel, or pitcher to pour batter onto surface. When suface begins to bubble, flip the pancake. Cook for 40 seconds, then remove from heating surface.

  3. Do the Whole Batch at Once

    Reaply butter or oil to surface ass needed.

  4. Enjoy!

    Consume promptly after cooking.
