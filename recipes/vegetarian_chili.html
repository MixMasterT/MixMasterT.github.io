Vegetarian Chili

  • Back to All Foods

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 chili pepper (optional)
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 and 1/2 tspoons baking powder
  • 2 cans of beans (black, pinto or both)
  • 1 small can or half-can of corn
  • 1 tspoon salt
  • 1/4 tspoon ground cumin (optional)

Description

Chili is a hearty and filling comfort food best enjoyed with warm tortillas and melted cheese.

Chili is also delicious served cold as a dip to accompany corn chips.

Directions

  1. Prepare Onions, Garlic and Peppers

    First, chop onion, garlic, and peppers. In large, oiled saucepan sautee onion first, then add in peppers, and garlic. Once aromatic, add cumin.

  2. Simmer

    Pour beans in over onion mixture. Add chopped tomato and corn in succession once hot. Simmer 5 - 10 minutes.

  3. Serve Warm

    Garnish with grated cheese. Vegetarian chili makes a great dinner when served with warm tortillas.

  4. Save for Later

    This chili can be refrigerated and reheats well in a microwave.
